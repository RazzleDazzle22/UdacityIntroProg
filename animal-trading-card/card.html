Beautiful Butterfly

Did you know that there are more than 17,500 recorded butterfly sepecies!

  • Scientific Name: Rhopalocera
  • Average Wingspan: 4 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 2 weeks
  • Lifecyles: 4 stages

Butterflies have been fluttering their little wings for over 65 million years! Adult butterflies have very large and colorful wings that help them camouflage; in order to escape predators. But, the reason butterflies are my favorite animal are because no two butterflies are alike!!
